04. Access GPUs

Get Access to GPUs

Once you've signed in to Google Cloud Platform, you can access GPUs. In the upper-left corner of the screen, click the menu-bar with three lines. Click IAM & Admin , then click Quotas .

If the following notification appears, indicating that the compute engine is getting ready, wait until it disappears.

Set Billing

After selecting Quotas , you may be prompted to Enable Billing. This is how you will link a project to your specific credits. Click Enable Billing and a pop-up should appear, indicating that your computer vision credits will be linked to this project. An example is shown below.

Finally, click the link that brings you to the "IAM & Admin Quotas" page. Next, you'll see how to define a quota and select a GPU.
